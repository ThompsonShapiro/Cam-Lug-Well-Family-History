Campbells, Luggs & Blackwells of Nelson, PA

Will Selph's 1924 chart, Genealogy of the Joseph Campbell Family, & 1935 Addendum - DRAFT


[1924 Chart]

As Will Selph was preparing to launch the Campbell Cousins Correspondence Club he assembled the family tree info he'd received from relatives and hired a draftsman to create this fantastic chart. He had copies made for each of his 19 remaining 1st cousins (out of the original 31); one for each of their 64 living children;and some extras for future distribution. The whole CCCC was only possible because the 31 grandchildren of Joseph & Ann Clinch Campbell all grew up in, or near, Nelson and knew each other. But by 1924 the "clan" was scattered from coast to coast. As with the CCC volumes, Will had the charts made, copied & mailed at his own expense (though some reimbursed him for the cost of the books).
